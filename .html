About Us

Kikuyu Boys High School was established in 1965 with a vision to provide quality education to young men. Our commitment to academic excellence and character development has made us a leading institution.

Our Mission

To provide holistic education that nurtures intellectual curiosity, creativity, and leadership in our students.

Academics

We offer a comprehensive curriculum designed to foster critical thinking and innovation. Our dedicated faculty is committed to helping students achieve their academic goals.

Subjects Offered

  • Mathematics
  • Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Languages (English, Kiswahili)
  • Humanities (History, Geography)
  • Technical Subjects (Computer Science, Business Studies)

Admissions

We welcome new students each year. Our admission process is straightforward and aims to provide every student an opportunity to join our community.
